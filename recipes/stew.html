Stew

Ingredients

  • Chilli
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Bell Pepper
  • Ginger
  • Chicken
  • Knorr cubes
  • Curry
  • Thyme
  • Water
  • Seasoning powder
  • salt
  • Tomatoe Paste

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken with added curry, knorr cubes, salt.
  2. Add some water to blender and; Blend the tomatoes, onions, chilli, bell pepper, ginger.(Dice some onions and set aside)
  3. Boil the blended mixture(pepper mix) till most of the water dissapears and the mix is thick.
  4. When chicken is boiled(try not to let the chicken get too soft), separate the chicken from the Chicken stock(boiled chicken water) and set aside.
  5. Fry the chicken with the Vegetable oil or you can airfry your chicken and set aside.
  6. By now your pepper mix should be thick and should have less water,set it aside.
  7. In a separate pot, pour some vegetable oil, then add your diced onions,let it fry a little bit, then add some tomatoe Paste,stir fry till you see it has some bubbles
  8. Add the pepper mix and also stir fry for about 5 minutes.
  9. Add the seasoning powder, curry, thyme,knorr cubes, salt.
  10. Stir fry for about a minute and then let it fry for about 2 minutes.
  11. Add the chicken stock and let the stew boil for about 5 minutes.
  12. Then add the fried chicken to the stew and let it boil for another 3 minutes and voila you have a delicious stew.

Nigerian chicken stew is the major meal that people eat in nigeria regardless of the tribe
